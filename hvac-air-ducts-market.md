<p><p>The HVAC air duct market comprises three primary types: sheet metal ducts, flexible non-metallic ducts, and fiberglass ducts. Sheet metal ducts are durable and efficient for high airflow systems. Flexible non-metallic ducts offer easy installation and adaptability in tight spaces, making them a popular choice for residential applications. Fiberglass ducts are known for their thermal insulation properties and sound absorption, ideal for quieter environments. Each type serves distinct needs in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems, influencing performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
