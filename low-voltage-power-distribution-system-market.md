<p><p>The Low Voltage Power Distribution System market comprises two primary types: Fixed Type and Drawer Type. Fixed Type systems are characterized by a stationary configuration that houses circuit breakers and distribution boards, making them reliable and efficient for stable applications. In contrast, Drawer Type systems feature modular compartments for easy access and maintenance, allowing components to be replaced or serviced without disrupting the entire system. Both types cater to diverse industrial and commercial needs, emphasizing safety and functionality in power distribution.</p></p>
