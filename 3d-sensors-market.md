<p><p>The 3D sensors market includes various types based on different sensing technologies. Image sensors capture 3D visual data, enabling high-resolution depth mapping for applications in robotics and augmented reality. Position sensors determine the spatial orientation and location of objects, commonly used in navigation and tracking systems. Accelerometers measure acceleration forces, aiding in motion detection and stabilization. Acoustic sensors utilize sound waves to gauge distance and create 3D maps, often employed in industrial and automotive applications for enhanced object recognition and safety.</p></p>
