<p><p>Intercoms are categorized into two main market types: wired and wireless. Wired intercoms utilize physical cables for audio and video transmission, offering stable connections and reliability, making them ideal for permanent installations in homes and businesses. Wireless intercoms, on the other hand, operate over radio frequencies or Wi-Fi, providing greater flexibility and ease of installation without extensive wiring. They are popular for temporary setups or areas where running cables is impractical, catering to diverse communication needs in various environments.</p></p>
