<p><p>Affective computing encompasses two primary market types: touch-based and touchless systems. Touch-based markets involve devices that require physical interaction, such as sensors that detect emotional responses through tactile engagement, like smartwatches or touchscreen devices. Touchless markets utilize non-contact technologies, such as facial recognition or gesture control, to interpret emotions from distance, enhancing user experience without direct interaction. Together, these markets aim to create more intuitive and responsive interfaces, allowing for deeper emotional engagement in technology.</p></p>
