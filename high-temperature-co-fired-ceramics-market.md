<p><p>The High-Temperature Co-fired Ceramics (HTCC) market primarily involves Al2O3 HTCC boards and AlN HTCC boards. Al2O3 HTCC boards, composed of aluminum oxide, are known for their excellent electrical insulation and thermal stability, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. In contrast, AlN HTCC boards, made from aluminum nitride, offer superior thermal conductivity and low dielectric loss, making them suitable for high-power applications. Both types serve critical roles in electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, driven by the demand for reliable and efficient components.</p></p>
