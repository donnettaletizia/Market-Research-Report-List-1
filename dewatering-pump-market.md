<p><strong>The Dewatering Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Dewatering Pumps</li><li>Non-Submersible Dewatering Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dewatering pump market is primarily divided into two types: submersible and non-submersible dewatering pumps. Submersible dewatering pumps are designed to operate underwater, effectively removing water and solids from submerged areas, making them ideal for construction sites, mines, and flooded areas. In contrast, non-submersible dewatering pumps are positioned above the water level, utilizing a pumping mechanism to draw water up from below. Both types serve critical roles in managing water in various industrial, municipal, and environmental applications.</p></p>
