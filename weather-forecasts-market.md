<p><p>Weather forecasts are categorized into three market types: short-range, medium-range, and long-range forecasts. Short-range forecasts typically cover a period of up to 48 hours, providing detailed insights into immediate weather conditions. Medium-range forecasts extend from 3 to 10 days, offering a broader outlook for planning purposes. Long-range forecasts project weather patterns for weeks to months ahead, focusing on general trends rather than specific conditions. Each type serves distinct needs, from day-to-day activities to seasonal planning.</p></p>
