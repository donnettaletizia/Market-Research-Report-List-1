<p><p>The outdoor mist cooling system market is categorized into three main types based on operating pressure. Low pressure systems operate around 40-100 psi, providing gentle mist suitable for smaller areas. Medium pressure systems, ranging from 100-500 psi, offer a balance of cooling efficiency and water conservation, making them ideal for larger spaces. High pressure systems operate above 500 psi, producing ultra-fine mist for rapid evaporation and significant cooling in expansive outdoor environments, enhancing comfort in hot climates.</p></p>
