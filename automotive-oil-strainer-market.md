<p><p>The Automotive Oil Strainer Market includes two main types: Floating Type and Fixed Type. Floating Type strainers are designed to move with the oil level, ensuring consistent filtration regardless of oil volume. They are ideal for applications with fluctuating oil levels. Fixed Type strainers remain stationary, providing stable filtration in systems with consistent oil levels. Both types are crucial for maintaining engine health by filtering contaminants, but their usage depends on the vehicle's design and operational requirements.</p></p>
