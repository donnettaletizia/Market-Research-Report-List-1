<p><ul><li>Electronic Toll Collection</li><li>All Electronic Toll Collection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Pay As You Go (PAYG) road charging encompasses two market types: Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) and All Electronic Toll Collection (AETC). ETC typically allows vehicles to pay tolls via electronic means at designated toll plazas, often using transponders for convenience. In contrast, AETC eliminates physical toll booths entirely, enabling seamless toll collection through automated vehicle recognition and communication systems on all road segments. Both systems aim to enhance efficiency, reduce congestion, and provide a user-friendly payment experience.</p></p>
