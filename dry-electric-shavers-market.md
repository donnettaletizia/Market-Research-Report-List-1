<p><p>The dry electric shavers market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and foil. Rotary shavers feature rotating cutters that easily navigate facial contours, making them ideal for thicker hair and providing a close shave. In contrast, foil shavers have oscillating blades behind a perforated metal foil, offering a smooth shave suited for finer hair types and sensitive skin. These distinct designs cater to different consumer preferences and grooming needs, driving segmentation and competition within the market.</p></p>
