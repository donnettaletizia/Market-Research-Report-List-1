<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT) are a popular flooring option, designed to mimic the appearance of natural materials like wood or stone. The LVT market is divided into two main types: Flexible LVT and Rigid LVT. Flexible LVT is characterized by its pliability, making it easier to install and adaptable to various subfloor conditions. In contrast, Rigid LVT features a stone-plastic composite core, offering enhanced durability and stability, making it suitable for high-traffic areas and environments requiring stronger performance.</p></p>
