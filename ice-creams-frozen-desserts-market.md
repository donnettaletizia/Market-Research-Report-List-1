<p><p>The ice cream and frozen desserts market is primarily divided into two categories: soft ice cream and hard ice cream. Soft ice cream has a lighter texture due to its higher air content and is typically served fresh from machines at restaurants or ice cream shops. In contrast, hard ice cream is churned at lower temperatures, resulting in a denser, creamier product that is stored in frozen tubs. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and are available in a variety of flavors and styles.</p></p>
