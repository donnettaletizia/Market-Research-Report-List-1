<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Gear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spur Gear</li><li>Bevel Gear</li><li>Screw Gear</li><li>Miter Gear</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive gear market includes various gear types essential for vehicle performance. **Spur gears** are simple, straight-toothed gears used for low-speed applications. **Bevel gears** have angled teeth, transmitting power between intersecting shafts, often in differentials. **Screw gears** operate at non-parallel, non-intersecting axes, offering smooth motion. **Miter gears** are specialized bevel gears with a 1:1 ratio, used for right-angle power transmission. **Others** include helical, worm, and planetary gears, each serving unique functions like torque distribution, noise reduction, or compact design in automotive systems.</p></p>
