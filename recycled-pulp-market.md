<p><ul><li>Mechanical Pulping</li><li>Chemical Pulping</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled pulp market is categorized into three main types: mechanical pulping, chemical pulping, and others. Mechanical pulping involves physically breaking down fibers, resulting in higher yield but reduced quality, often used for newsprint and packaging. Chemical pulping employs chemical processes to dissolve lignin, yielding stronger and higher-quality pulp suitable for premium paper products. The "others" category encompasses hybrid and alternative methods, which may combine techniques or use non-traditional materials, catering to niche applications and sustainability goals.</p></p>
