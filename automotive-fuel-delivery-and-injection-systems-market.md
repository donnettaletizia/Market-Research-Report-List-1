<p><p>The automotive fuel delivery and injection systems market encompasses various technologies designed to optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency. Throttle body fuel injectors deliver fuel into the intake manifold, aiding in air-fuel mixing. Direct fuel injectors inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber, improving efficiency and reducing emissions. Sequential fuel injectors operate by injecting fuel at specific times for each cylinder, enhancing power and efficiency. Port fuel injectors spray fuel into the intake ports, promoting better atomization and combustion.</p></p>
