<p><ul><li>Plate-and-Block HE</li><li>Plate-and-Shell HE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fully welded plate heat exchangers are key in thermal management across various industries, with two main types: plate-and-block heat exchangers and plate-and-shell heat exchangers. Plate-and-block HE features a compact design with welded plates arranged in a block, optimizing heat transfer and minimizing pressure drops, suitable for high-viscosity fluids. In contrast, plate-and-shell HE consists of plates housed within a shell, providing robust construction ideal for high-pressure applications, featuring enhanced flexibility and efficiency in heat transfer processes.</p></p>
