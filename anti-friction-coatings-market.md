<p><p>The anti-friction coatings market is categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, offering lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them suitable for various applications. They provide good adhesion and performance while being safer for users and the environment. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings typically offer superior durability and heat resistance, making them ideal for demanding industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs, with water-based options emphasizing sustainability and solvent-based choices prioritizing performance.</p></p>
