<p><strong>The Aluminum Foam for Construction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cell Aluminum Foam</li><li>Closed Cell Aluminum Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum foam is a lightweight, versatile material used in construction, characterized by its structure of air-filled cells. Open cell aluminum foam features interconnected pores, offering excellent energy absorption and sound insulation, making it suitable for applications like acoustic panels and lightweight structural support. In contrast, closed cell aluminum foam has individually sealed cells, providing superior thermal insulation, buoyancy, and structural integrity, ideal for applications requiring corrosion resistance and impact protection, such as in facades and protective barriers.</p></p>
