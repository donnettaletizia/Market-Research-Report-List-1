<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Structural Steel Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Tube</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW) Tube</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld (ERW) Tube</li><li>Seamless (SMLS) Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural steel tube market comprises several types based on welding and manufacturing processes. Spiral weld tubes are created by spirally welding flat steel strips, providing strength for large diameters. Double submerged arc weld (DSAW) tubes use dual arcs for thicker walls and durability. Electric resistance weld (ERW) tubes are produced by welding parallel edges of steel sheets using electrical resistance, suitable for various applications. Seamless (SMLS) tubes are manufactured without joints, offering high pressure and stress resistance, ideal for critical applications.</p></p>
