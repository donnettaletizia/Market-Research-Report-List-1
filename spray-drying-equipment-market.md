<p><p>Spray drying equipment is categorized into single-stage, two-stage, and multi-stage systems based on their operational complexity and efficiency. Single-stage systems involve a single drying chamber where feed material is converted to powder, suitable for less complex applications. Two-stage systems feature a secondary chamber for further drying, enhancing product quality and reducing moisture content. Multi-stage systems incorporate multiple drying stages, offering higher efficiency and improved product characteristics, making them ideal for more demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
