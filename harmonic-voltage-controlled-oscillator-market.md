<p><p>The Harmonic Voltage Controlled Oscillator (HVCO) market can be categorized into three main types. Crystal Oscillators employ quartz crystals for precise frequency stability, commonly used in communication devices. The "Other" market includes a variety of emerging technologies such as MEMS oscillators and digitally controlled oscillators, catering to specific applications where traditional designs may not meet requirements. Together, these segments drive innovation in oscillator technologies.</p></p>
