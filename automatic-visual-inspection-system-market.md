<p><p>The Automatic Visual Inspection (AVI) system market is primarily categorized into front inspection and back inspection. Front inspection focuses on evaluating the visible surfaces of products, identifying defects and ensuring quality before they are packaged or delivered. In contrast, back inspection targets the hidden or reverse sides of products, often assessing components that are not easily visible during front inspection. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring product quality and reliability in manufacturing processes across various industries.</p></p>
