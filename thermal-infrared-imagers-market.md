<p><p>The thermal infrared imagers market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal imaging imagers. Uncooled thermal imagers operate at ambient temperatures without the need for cooling mechanisms, making them compact and cost-effective, ideal for applications like building inspection and surveillance. Cooled thermal imagers, on the other hand, utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and image quality, suitable for more demanding applications such as military, aerospace, and scientific research, where high precision is crucial.</p></p>
