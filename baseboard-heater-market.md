<p><strong>The Baseboard Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baseboard heater market is primarily divided into two types: electric and hydronic heaters. Electric baseboard heaters operate using electricity to generate heat, offering easy installation and maintenance. They are typically ideal for smaller spaces or individual rooms. In contrast, hydronic baseboard heaters utilize heated water from a boiler, providing more efficient and consistent warmth. This type is often favored in larger homes due to its energy efficiency and ability to heat entire spaces more evenly.</p></p>
