<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Advanced Semiconductor Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP)</li><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP)</li><li>Flip Chip (FC)</li><li>2.5D/3D</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced semiconductor packaging market comprises several technologies that enhance performance and miniaturization. Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP) offers compact designs with better thermal performance. Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP) allows for high-density connections but has limitations in size. Flip Chip (FC) technology enables direct attachment of chips to substrates, improving electrical performance. 2.5D and 3D packaging integrate multiple chips vertically or horizontally, enhancing connectivity and reducing space. Other methods include System-in-Package (SiP) and package-on-package (PoP), catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
