<p><strong>The Smart Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Meter Reading (AMR)</li><li>Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Gas Meter market encompasses two primary types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR allows for remote data collection from gas meters, enabling utility companies to efficiently monitor consumption without manual readings. In contrast, AMI represents a more sophisticated system that not only facilitates remote reading but also supports two-way communication between the meter and the utility. This allows for real-time data analysis, enhanced customer insights, and improved demand management in gas distribution.</p></p>
