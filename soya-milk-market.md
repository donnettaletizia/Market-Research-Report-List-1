<p><p>The soya milk market is divided into conventional and organic segments. Conventional soya milk is produced using genetically modified or non-GMO soybeans with synthetic fertilizers and pesticides, making it cost-effective and widely available. Organic soya milk, on the other hand, is made from non-GMO soybeans grown without synthetic chemicals, adhering to strict organic farming standards. It appeals to health-conscious consumers seeking natural, eco-friendly products. Both types cater to lactose-intolerant and vegan diets, but organic options often command a premium due to their perceived health and environmental benefits.</p></p>
