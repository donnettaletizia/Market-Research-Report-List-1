<p><strong>The Chemisorption analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Flow Chemisorption Analyzer</li><li>Static Chemisorption Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemisorption analyzer market comprises two primary types: dynamic flow and static chemisorption analyzers. Dynamic flow chemisorption analyzers continuously pass reactant gases over the sample, enabling real-time monitoring of adsorption dynamics and kinetics. In contrast, static chemisorption analyzers involve a closed system where reactant gases interact with the sample without external flow, allowing for precise measurement of adsorption characteristics at equilibrium. Both types are essential for characterizing surface properties and catalytic activity of materials in various applications.</p></p>
