<p><p>The Concrete Sound Barrier market is divided into two primary types: Ground-Mounted and Structure-Mounted barriers. Ground-Mounted barriers are installed directly on the ground and are effective for reducing noise from roadways or railways, often featuring taller designs for maximum sound absorption. In contrast, Structure-Mounted barriers are attached to existing structures, such as buildings or bridges, serving to mitigate noise in urban areas. Both types are vital for enhancing acoustic comfort in residential and commercial spaces, contributing to public health and well-being.</p></p>
