<p><strong>The Analgesic Infusion Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Patient-Controlled Analgesia Pump (PCA Pump)</li><li>Intrathecal Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analgesic infusion pumps are devices that deliver pain relief medication directly to patients. The Patient-Controlled Analgesia Pump (PCA Pump) allows patients to self-administer predetermined doses of analgesics, enhancing comfort and ensuring timely pain relief. In contrast, intrathecal pumps deliver medication directly into the cerebrospinal fluid, providing targeted pain control for chronic conditions. Both types are crucial in managing pain effectively, improving patient outcomes, and minimizing the reliance on oral or systemic pain medications.</p></p>
