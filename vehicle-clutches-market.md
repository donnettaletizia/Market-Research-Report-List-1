<p><p>The vehicle clutches market includes friction clutches and electromagnetic clutches. Friction clutches, the most common type, use friction plates to engage and disengage power transmission, offering durability and reliability for manual transmissions. Electromagnetic clutches, on the other hand, use magnetic fields to control engagement, providing smoother operation and precise control, often used in automated systems. Both types cater to diverse automotive needs, balancing performance, efficiency, and application-specific requirements in vehicles ranging from passenger cars to heavy-duty machinery.</p></p>
