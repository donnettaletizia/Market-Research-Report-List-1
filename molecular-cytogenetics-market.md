<p><strong>The Molecular Cytogenetics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Radioactive In Situ Hybridization</li><li>Comparative Genomic Hybridization</li><li>In Situ Hybridization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular cytogenetics market comprises several key types. Non-Radioactive In Situ Hybridization (NISH) utilizes fluorescent or enzymatic labels for visualizing chromosomal abnormalities without the use of radiation. Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) analyzes genomic differences between samples, aiding in identifying copy number variations and genomic imbalances. In Situ Hybridization (ISH) employs a diverse range of labeled probes to detect specific nucleic acid sequences within fixed cells or tissues, providing valuable insights for diagnostics and research applications in genetics and oncology.</p></p>
