<p><p>Extended Life Coolant (ELC) and antifreeze are essential fluids used in automotive and industrial applications to regulate engine temperature, prevent overheating, and protect against freezing in cold climates. ELC is a type of coolant designed for extended service intervals, often lasting up to 1 million miles or more, due to its advanced formulation with organic acid technology (OAT) or hybrid organic acid technology (HOAT). Antifreeze, on the other hand, is a more traditional coolant that requires more frequent replacement and typically contains ethylene glycol or propylene glycol.</p><p>The Extended Life Coolant (ELC) and Antifreeze Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for high-performance vehicles, industrial machinery, and the need for efficient thermal management systems. Additionally, the expansion of the automotive and industrial sectors in emerging economies is fueling market demand.
