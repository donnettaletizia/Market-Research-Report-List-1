<p><strong>The Highly Transparent Low Iron Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The highly transparent low iron glass market is primarily categorized into rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating thinner sheets that offer excellent transparency and uniformity. This type is often used in applications requiring specific shapes or thicknesses. Float glass, on the other hand, is made by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a smooth, flat surface ideal for architectural and automotive uses. Both types ensure optimal light transmission and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
