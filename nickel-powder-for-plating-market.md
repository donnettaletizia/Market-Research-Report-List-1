<p><p>The nickel powder for plating market primarily consists of two types: carbonyl nickel powder and electrolytic nickel powder. Carbonyl nickel powder, produced through carbonylation, offers high purity and fine particle size, making it ideal for electroplating applications where precision and uniformity are critical. In contrast, electrolytic nickel powder is derived from electrolysis, resulting in larger particles with excellent flowability and a coarser texture, suitable for various industrial plating processes. Both types serve distinct applications in protective and decorative plating.</p></p>
