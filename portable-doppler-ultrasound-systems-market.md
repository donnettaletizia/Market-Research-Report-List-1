<p><ul><li>Directional Doppler</li><li>Non-Directional Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Doppler Ultrasound Systems market is categorized into two main types: Directional Doppler and Non-Directional Doppler. Directional Doppler systems focus on determining the speed and direction of blood flow by analyzing frequency shifts in ultrasound waves. In contrast, Non-Directional Doppler systems measure the overall presence and intensity of blood flow without providing directional specifics. Both types play essential roles in medical diagnostics, offering clinicians portable tools for efficient and effective patient assessments in various settings.</p></p>
