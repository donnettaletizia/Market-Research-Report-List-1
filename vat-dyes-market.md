<p><p>Vat dyes are a class of dyes primarily used for coloring cellulosic fibers like cotton, linen, and rayon. Known for their excellent colorfastness, lightfastness, and resistance to washing, vat dyes are insoluble in water and require a reduction process to make them soluble for application. Once applied, they are oxidized back to their insoluble form, ensuring long-lasting and vibrant colors. These dyes are widely used in the textile industry for high-quality fabrics, including denim, workwear, and home textiles.</p><p>The Vat Dyes Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for durable and eco-friendly dyes in the textile industry.
