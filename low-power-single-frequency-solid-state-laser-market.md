<p><p>The Low Power Single Frequency Solid State Laser market is categorized into two main types: Pulse Type and Continuous Wave (CW) Type. Pulse Type lasers produce short bursts of light energy, making them ideal for applications requiring high peak power and precise energy delivery, such as material processing and medical procedures. Meanwhile, Continuous Wave (CW) Type lasers emit a constant beam of light, suitable for applications like telecommunications and precision measurements, where stable and uninterrupted output is essential.</p></p>
