<p><p>The air sander market includes various types, prominently featuring orbital and belt sanders. Orbital sanders use a circular sanding pad that moves in random orbits, providing a smooth finish on wood and surfaces without leaving swirl marks. In contrast, belt sanders utilize a continuous loop of sandpaper for aggressive material removal, ideal for leveling and shaping wood surfaces quickly. Both types cater to different sanding needs, making them essential tools in woodworking and construction industries.</p></p>
