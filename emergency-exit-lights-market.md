<p><ul><li>Maintained</li><li>Sustained(Non-maintained)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency and exit lights market can be categorized into two primary types: maintained and sustained (non-maintained) systems. Maintained lights are illuminated continuously, providing ongoing visibility and guidance during power outages. In contrast, sustained lights remain off under normal conditions and only activate during emergencies, conserving energy and extending bulb life. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring safety and compliance in buildings, aiding evacuation during emergencies while addressing varying energy efficiency and operational needs.</p></p>
