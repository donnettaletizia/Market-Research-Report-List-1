Plastics printing leverages photopolymer resins to produce lightweight, versatile components ideal for prototyping and end-use products. Ceramics printing focuses on high-temperature materials for applications in art, dental, and engineering sectors, providing robust, heat-resistant solutions that meet diverse industry needs. Each segment contributes to the evolving landscape of additive manufacturing.</p></p>
