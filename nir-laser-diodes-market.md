<p><p>The NIR laser diodes market is categorized into single-mode and multi-mode types. Multi-mode laser diodes, on the other hand, support multiple light paths, making them suitable for short-distance applications like data centers and industrial sensors. Each type serves distinct needs in telecommunications, medical devices, and sensing technologies, driving growth in various sectors.</p></p>
