<p><p>The cardiology electrodes market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Wet gel electrodes utilize a conductive gel to enhance signal quality, typically used during diagnostics. Dry electrodes eliminate the need for conductive substances, providing convenience for routine checks. Solid gel electrodes maintain stable conductivity without drying out, making them suitable for various electrocardiographic applications. Each type serves unique patient needs and procedural requirements.</p></p>
