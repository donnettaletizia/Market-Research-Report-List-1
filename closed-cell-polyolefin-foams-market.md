<p><p>The closed cell polyolefin foams market includes two primary types: Polyethylene (PE) compound and PE/EVA blends. Polyethylene foam is known for its lightweight, durability, and excellent thermal insulation properties, making it ideal for packaging, automotive, and construction applications. On the other hand, PE/EVA foam combines polyethylene with ethylene-vinyl acetate, enhancing flexibility and elasticity, which is particularly useful in cushioning, soundproofing, and flooring solutions. Both types offer diverse functionalities across various industries, driven by their unique material properties.</p></p>
