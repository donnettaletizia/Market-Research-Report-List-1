<p><strong>The Monocular Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Night Vision</li><li>Thermal imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monocular camera market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on night vision and thermal imaging technologies. Night vision cameras amplify available light, enabling visibility in low-light conditions, making them ideal for surveillance and outdoor activities at night. In contrast, thermal imaging cameras detect heat patterns, allowing users to see through obscurants like fog or smoke, and are commonly utilized in search and rescue, security, and industrial applications. Both technologies enhance situational awareness, catering to diverse user needs in various environments.</p></p>
