<p><p>The automatic swimming pool cleaner market is primarily categorized into two types: crawler drive and wheel drive systems. Crawler drive cleaners utilize continuous tracks for improved traction and maneuverability over uneven surfaces, making them effective for various pool shapes and surfaces. Conversely, wheel drive cleaners use wheels for movement, offering faster navigation but may be less effective on rough textures. Both types aim to provide efficient cleaning solutions, enhancing convenience for pool owners while maintaining optimal water quality.</p></p>
