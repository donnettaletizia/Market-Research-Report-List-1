Regenerative thermal oxidizers recycle heat to minimize energy consumption while maximizing destruction efficiency. Regenerative catalytic oxidizers combine both regenerative and catalytic principles, offering advanced pollutant removal while conserving energy, making them suitable for high-volume emission sources.</p></p>
