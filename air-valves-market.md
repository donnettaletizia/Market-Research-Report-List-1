<p><strong>The Air Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Release Valves</li><li>Air/Vacuum Valves</li><li>Combination Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air valves market consists of three main types: Air-Release Valves, Air/Vacuum Valves, and Combination Valves. Air-Release Valves are designed to release trapped air in pipelines, enhancing efficiency and preventing pressure surges. Air/Vacuum Valves allow air to enter or exit the system, preventing vacuum conditions and cavitation. Combination Valves integrate the functions of both, enabling effective air management. These valves are essential in various industries, including water supply, wastewater treatment, and irrigation, ensuring optimal system performance and reliability.</p></p>
