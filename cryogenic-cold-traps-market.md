<p><ul><li>Embedded Cold Trap</li><li>Split Cold Trap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryogenic cold traps market features two primary types: embedded cold traps and split cold traps. Embedded cold traps are integrated within vacuum systems, efficiently capturing gaseous contaminants and ensuring sample purity during cryogenic processes. In contrast, split cold traps are standalone units that can be connected to various systems, allowing for flexibility and easier maintenance. Both types play crucial roles in laboratories and industrial applications, enhancing the performance of cryogenic systems by preventing unwanted vapor escape and facilitating better operational efficiency.</p></p>
