<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof night vision goggles market comprises two main types: image intensifier and thermal imaging devices. Image intensifier goggles amplify ambient light to enhance visibility in low-light conditions, making them ideal for nighttime use. In contrast, thermal imaging goggles detect heat signatures, allowing users to see in complete darkness by visualizing temperature differences. Both technologies serve various applications, including military, law enforcement, and outdoor activities, providing crucial visibility and situational awareness in challenging environments.</p></p>
