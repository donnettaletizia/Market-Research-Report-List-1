<p><p>RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) tags and labels are technology-based tools used for automatic identification and data capture. <p><p>The RFID tags and labels market is categorized into three main types: RFID paper labels, RFID plastic labels, and RFID hybrid labels. RFID paper labels are lightweight and cost-effective, ideal for short-term use or applications like inventory management. RFID plastic labels are more durable and weather-resistant, suitable for outdoor use or harsh environments. RFID hybrid labels combine features of both paper and plastic, offering versatility for various applications, including logistics and retail, ensuring efficient tracking and data collection.</p></p>
