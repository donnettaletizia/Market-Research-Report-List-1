<p><p>A horizontal centrifugal pump is a type of pump that uses rotational energy, typically from an electric motor, to move fluids through dynamic mechanisms. It comprises a rotor that imparts kinetic energy to the fluid, allowing it to flow outward and into a discharge manifold. This design enables efficient movement of liquids and is commonly used in various applications, including water supply, irrigation, and chemical processing.</p><p>The Horizontal Centrifugal Pump Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand across various industries, particularly in water and wastewater treatment, HVAC systems, and process industries.
