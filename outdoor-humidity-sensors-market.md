<p><p>The outdoor humidity sensor market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless sensors. Wired outdoor humidity sensors are connected directly to a central unit via cables, offering reliable data transmission, especially in fixed installations. They are typically more durable but can be challenging to install in remote locations. In contrast, wireless outdoor humidity sensors transmit data using radio signals or Wi-Fi, allowing for flexible installation and easier relocation. They provide convenience and accessibility but may be affected by battery life and signal range.</p></p>
