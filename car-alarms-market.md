<p><ul><li>Passive Car Alarm</li><li>Active Car Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car alarms market is primarily divided into two types: passive and active car alarms. Passive car alarms automatically engage when the vehicle is turned off, providing continuous protection without user action. These systems often include features like door lock sensors and ignition kill switches. In contrast, active car alarms require user activation, typically involving a remote control to arm or disarm. Both types aim to enhance vehicle security, deter theft, and protect the owner's investment in their vehicle.</p></p>
