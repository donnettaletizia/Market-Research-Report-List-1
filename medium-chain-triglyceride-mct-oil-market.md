<p><p>Medium Chain Triglyceride (MCT) oil is a dietary supplement derived from coconut oil or palm kernel oil, consisting primarily of medium-chain fatty acids such as caprylic and capric acid. The benefits of MCT oil include enhanced energy levels, improved cognitive function, and potential weight management support.</p><p>The MCT oil market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing health consciousness among consumers and rising demand in the food and beverage, dietary supplements, and personal care industries.
