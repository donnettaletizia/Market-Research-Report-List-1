<p><p>Fire retardant epoxy systems are categorized into several market types based on their chemical composition and properties. Halogenated fire retardants incorporate halogens to effectively inhibit fire spread. Inorganic fire retardants, derived from minerals, rely on cooling effects and non-flammable characteristics. Organic phosphorus fire retardants function by forming a protective char when exposed to heat, while nitrogenous fire retardants utilize nitrogen compounds to reduce flammability and promote heat dissipation. Each type offers unique benefits for specific applications and industry requirements.</p></p>
