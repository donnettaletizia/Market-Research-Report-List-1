<p><p>The medical pressure infusion equipment market is divided into two main types: manual pressure infusion and automatic pressure infusion. Manual pressure infusors require healthcare professionals to apply pressure manually to deliver fluids, offering flexibility and control during critical situations. In contrast, automatic pressure infusors are device-driven, providing consistent, regulated pressure to ensure precise fluid delivery, reducing the risk of human error. Both types are essential in various medical settings, enhancing patient care and treatment efficiency.</p></p>
