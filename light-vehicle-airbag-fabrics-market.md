<p><p>The Light Vehicle Airbag Fabrics market comprises two main types: Flat Airbag Fabric and OPW (Open Parenthesis Woven) fabric. Flat Airbag Fabric is primarily used for traditional airbags, offering a durable, yet lightweight design that ensures effective deployment and protection. OPW fabric, on the other hand, features an innovative woven structure that enhances air retention and rapid inflation, making it suitable for advanced airbag systems. Both fabric types are essential for improving vehicle safety and occupant protection in light vehicles.</p></p>
