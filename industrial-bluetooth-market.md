<p><p>The Industrial Bluetooth market is primarily categorized into single-mode and dual-mode modules. Single-mode modules support only Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), making them ideal for applications focused on low power consumption and reduced data transmission rates, such as sensor networks. In contrast, dual-mode modules support both classic Bluetooth and BLE, offering versatility for applications that require higher data rates and the ability to connect with a wider range of devices, such as audio and data streaming in industrial environments.</p></p>
