<p><p>Airborne LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) is a remote sensing technology that employs laser light to measure distances from an aircraft to the Earth's surface. This technology has seen increasing adoption across various sectors such as forestry, agriculture, urban planning, and environmental monitoring.</p><p>The Airborne LiDAR Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period, driven by advancements in technology, the rising demand for accurate geospatial data, and the growing applications in sectors such as defense, transportation, and asset management. Additionally, the development of lightweight, cost-efficient sensors is enabling broader accessibility for small to medium enterprises, significantly expanding the market. The demand for high-resolution mapping and 3D modeling in urban areas continues to rise, further propelling market expansion.
