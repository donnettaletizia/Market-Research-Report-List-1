<p><p>The taste modulators market consists of three primary types: sweet modulators, salt modulators, and fat modulators. Sweet modulators enhance sweetness without adding sugar, making them ideal for low-calorie products. Salt modulators reduce sodium content while maintaining a salty flavor, catering to health-conscious consumers. Fat modulators mimic the mouthfeel of fat, allowing for creamy textures in reduced-fat products. Together, these modulators enable food manufacturers to create healthier options without compromising taste, appealing to the growing demand for better-for-you foods.</p></p>
